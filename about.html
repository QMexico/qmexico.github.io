About Us

Quantum Mexico is a community that brings quantum enthusiasts around the country together. Researchers, professionals and students of diverse backgrounds can all become part of the community and work together to popularize quantum technologies in Mexico. Our country is full of promising individuals who can contribute to the area, this is the place where they can learn more and form new relationships that will boost their carreer. We are currently working on a series of webinars with awesome people, stay tuned!

Our Values

  • Sense of community: We want to offer a friendly environment in which all members can develop and work together to reach both community-wide and personal goals.
  • Professionalism: As a community dedicated to STEM, we're committed to sharing truthful, unbiased information which has been verified by experts in the area.
  • Tolerance and inclusivity: We are a discrimination-free community. All members are treated equally regardless of gender, race, ethnicity, age or any other defining characteristic.

Our Objectives

  • To motivate and help people in our country, regardless of their background, to get involved in quantum technologies.
  • To help anyone who wishes to pursue a carreer in quantum by offering education tools as well as networking with professionals and other quantum enthusiasts around the world.
  • To reduce technological illiteracy and fear of society to get invovled in the areas of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM).

We are seeking to become part of the QWorld network within this year!

QMexico stands for science, compromise and the momentum to promote fundamental concepts with high scientific rigor. We believe in science as a driver for societal advancement.
